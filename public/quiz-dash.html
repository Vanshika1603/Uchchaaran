FEEDBACK


Articulation Accuracy: Assess if target sounds are produced correctly, calculating the percentage of correct productions.
Phonetic Transcription: Document and analyze specific phonetic errors using IPA symbols.
Error Types: Categorize errors like substitution, distortion, omission, and addition.
Phonological Processes: Analyze patterns like final consonant deletion, cluster reduction, and stopping.
Speech Intelligibility: Measure how well others understand the speaker, using metrics like Percentage of Consonants Correct (PCC).
Stimulability: Determine if the individual can produce the target sound with cues or instruction.
Contextual Variability: Analyze errors in specific word positions and contexts.
Speech Rate and Fluency: Measure speech rate and fluency disruptions.
Voice Onset Time (VOT) and Voice Quality: Assess parameters for specific speech sounds.
Syllable Structure and Stress Patterns: Analyze syllable structure and stress.
Progress Monitoring: Regularly assess progress to evaluate therapy effectiveness.
Subjective Ratings: Gather feedback from individuals, caregivers, or educators to understand the impact of misarticulation.
